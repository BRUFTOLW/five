Frequently asked questions

Straight answers to the most common questions about working with Northlight.

Do you bring supplies and equipment?

Yes. We supply professional tools and neutral cleaners; eco-options on request. If you prefer your products, please share SDS or the container label for safety.

Are you insured and covered by WCB?

Yes—liability insurance and WCB coverage. We can provide certificates on request. Your building or property managers can be added as certificate holders.

Do you charge sales tax (RST/PST) on house cleaning?

In Manitoba, most janitorial services performed to real property are RST-exempt. Cleaning taxable goods or selling products is different. We follow the provincial rules on each invoice.

What is your minimum charge?

There is a 2 labour-hour minimum per visit to protect fair travel and setup time.

Do you serve evenings or weekends?

Yes. Monday–Thursday evenings before 9 pm plus limited Saturday evening slots.
