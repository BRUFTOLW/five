Transparent pricing

Hourly range

$42–$48 per labour-hour per cleaner. Built from loaded labour cost with a 40–45% gross margin target.

  • Solo (2h minimum) ≈ $90–100 per visit
  • Two-person team (2 labour-hours total) ≈ $180–200 minimum
  • Residential terms: Net 7 days
  • Commercial pilot terms: Net 15 days

Late cancel policy

24h notice required or $50 late-cancel fee. Waived on the first miss.

Eco options

How booking works

  1. Request estimate. Share address, size, priorities, timing.
  2. Quick walkthrough. In-person or virtual to confirm scope.
  3. Same-day quote. Written inclusions, exclusions, and visit minimum.
  4. First clean scheduled. Reminder sent 24 hours before.
  5. Quality pass. On-site review; issue log if re-work is needed.
  6. Invoice + feedback. Secure payment link; review request after visit 1–2.
