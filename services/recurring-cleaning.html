Recurring Home Cleaning

For apartments, condos, townhomes, and single-family homes needing consistent resets.

What's included every visit

  • Kitchens: surfaces, cabinet fronts, sinks, appliance exteriors, floors
  • Bathrooms: toilets, tubs/showers, vanities, mirrors, floors
  • Dusting: surfaces, ledges, fixtures reachable by 2-step stool
  • Floors: vacuum plus damp mop
  • Bedrooms and living spaces: tidy, surfaces, floors

Popular upsells

  • Inside fridge or oven
  • Baseboards and door detailing
  • Balcony sweep (seasonal)

Time guides

  • 600–900 sq ft: 2–3 labour-hours
  • 900–1400 sq ft: 3–4 labour-hours
  • 1400–2200 sq ft: 4–6 labour-hours

Visit minimum

2 labour-hour minimum to protect fair travel and setup time.

Exclusions

  • Biohazard or hoarding conditions
  • Exterior windows above reachable height
  • Paint or renovation debris removal

Keep your home on schedule

Book weekly, biweekly, or monthly. Start within 3–7 days after your estimate.
