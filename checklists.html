Cleaning checklists

Every visit uses a standardized checklist. Download and see exactly what we cover.

Recurring home cleaning

  • Kitchens: counters, sinks, exterior appliances, floors
  • Bathrooms: toilets, tubs/showers, vanities, mirrors
  • Dusting: surfaces, ledges, reachable fixtures
  • Floors: vacuum and damp mop
  • Bedrooms & living spaces: tidy, surfaces, floors

Upsells: inside fridge/oven, baseboards/doors detail, balcony sweep (seasonal).

Move-in / Move-out & deep cleaning

  • All recurring scope
  • Baseboards, trim, doors, reachable vents
  • Cabinet interiors (empty)
  • Inside fridge and oven on request
  • High-touch detailing on switches and handles

Exclusions: garbage hauling, construction waste, exterior windows.

Small commercial pilot

  • Reception and waiting areas reset
  • Disinfect high-touch points
  • Restrooms cleaned and restocked (client-supplied)
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Waste and recycling staged for pickup

Proof: checklist sign-off per visit + issue log with 24h response.
